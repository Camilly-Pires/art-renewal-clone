About the Art Renewal Center

The Art Renewal Center (ARC) is the largest online museum dedicated to representational art, with over 100,000 images.

Founded in 1999, ARC advocates for a return of skill-based training in the visual arts through education, exhibitions, and competitions.

We believe that great art is based on skill, beauty, and timeless values that transcend cultures and generations.

Our History

The ARC was established to counteract the decline of traditional art education and to preserve the techniques of the Old Masters.

Over the years, we've grown into a global community of artists, collectors, and art enthusiasts who share our vision.
